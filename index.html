I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at the University of Arkansas. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Rochester.

My research focuses on macroeconomics, banking, and international finance, with particular interest in financial intermediation and the role of banks in the transmission of aggregate shocks.
