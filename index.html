About

I am a Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Economics at the University of Arkansas. I received my Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Rochester.

I work in macroeconomics, banking, and international finance, with a focus on financial intermediation and the transmission of shocks through banks’ balance sheets.
